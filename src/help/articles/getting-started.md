# Getting started

Welcome to Tickd. Five minutes here will save you an hour of poking.

## What's on the screen

When you sign in, the **Today** view is the first thing you see. It's a snapshot of what's due, what's in progress, and what's coming up — built from the tasks you PIC ("Person In Charge") or watch.

![Today view with three zones: Needs attention, In progress, This week](/help/today-overview.png)

The sidebar on the left is your home base for moving between views:

- **Today** — the dashboard
- **List** — every task as rows, with filters and bulk actions
- **Grid** — table view; best for editing many fields at once
- **By PIC** — every person's open work, one column at a time
- **Calendar** — week / month grid
- **Docs** — your notes and references
- **Inbox** — updates, mentions, nudges (where Tickd pulls things to your attention)

On mobile the sidebar collapses to a bottom nav with the most-used views; the rest sit behind the "More" menu.

## Adding your first task

The blue **+ New task** button in the sidebar opens a quick-entry box anywhere you are.

![+ New task button in the sidebar](/help/new-task-button.png)

Type a title and press Enter. That's the minimum. You can also do all of this in one line:

- Type `@Lara` to assign it to a teammate (the autocomplete suggests names)
- Type `#strategy` to tag it
- Type a date like `Mon` or `next Friday` and Tickd will set the due date

Once it's saved, click the task row anywhere to open the full editor — that's where you set priority, watchers, notes, and the rest.

> [!TIP]
> The keyboard shortcut **/** (forward slash) jumps your cursor into the quick-entry box from anywhere. Esc closes anything that's open.
